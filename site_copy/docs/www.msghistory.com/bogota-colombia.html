Photo 1: (Source Danny Baker) The local civilians were assigned to follow us to provide extra security, during our transit to and from the embassy. The photo was taken outside the front of the Marine House.
Pictured kneeling front is Sgt Rich Rohde, picture standing in the back from left to right, Sgt Cliff (JD) Daniels and Sgt Rick Kondash, The obscure Marine I believe is Sgt Samuel Romero.
Photo 2: From left to right three civilians were local security. Sgt Terry Sheffer center, Sgt Danny L. Baker and finally our driver Jose (last name unk). Just a tidbit of information Ed came down with a crew from Leatherneck and interviewed the Marines. Two weeks prior to my arrival one of the drug cartels set off a car bomb in front of the embassy. Our liberty was restricted for the next six months. The car that was used was an old 1952 Chevy that was pretty much intact after the explosion. Approximately a month later I was snooping around on top of the embassy roof and found a piece of the car😂 They don't make them the way that they used to.
